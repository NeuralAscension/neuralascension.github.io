Intro

After receiving numerous requests for specialized neuroscience and engineering support throughout our doctoral training and into postdoctoral fellowships, NeuralAscension was founded to extend the reach of these skillsets beyond word-of-mouth within the Texas Medical Center and our close collaborators. Our aim is to address a critical gap within the neuroscience and neurotech communities: the need for specialized support in data acquisition, efficient signal processing, embedded systems, data analytics/science/machine learning, mathematical modeling, animal procedures, clinical applications, and more.

We offer solutions with our multidisciplinary team of doctoral-level electrical and computer engineers, neuroscientists with medical training bringing over three decades of combined, hands-on neuroscience research and clinical experience.
